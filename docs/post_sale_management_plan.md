# Plan for Post-Sale Customer Management Features

## Objective
Enhance customer retention and satisfaction through dedicated post-sale management tools.

## Features:
1. **Customer Success Dashboard**:
   - Track onboarding, adoption, and renewal status.
   - Assign customer success managers.

2. **Support Ticketing**:
   - Integrated support ticket system.
   - Link tickets to customer records and deals.

3. **Renewal and Upsell Management**:
   - Automated reminders for renewals.
   - Identify upsell/cross-sell opportunities.

4. **Satisfaction Surveys**:
   - Send NPS and CSAT surveys.
   - Analyze feedback and trends.

## Technical Stack
- **Frontend**: React.js for dashboards and ticketing UI.
- **Backend**: API endpoints for post-sale processes.
- **Integrations**: Email, survey tools, and calendar APIs.

## Deliverables
- Customer success dashboard.
- Support ticketing and survey modules.
- Documentation for users and admins.

## Timeline
- **Week 1**: Design data model and UI.
- **Week 2**: Develop backend and integrations.
- **Week 3**: Build frontend and reporting features.
- **Week 4**: Test, deploy, and document.
