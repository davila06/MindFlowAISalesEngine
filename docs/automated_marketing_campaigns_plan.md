# Plan for Automated Marketing Campaigns with Segmentation

## Objective
Enable users to create, automate, and analyze marketing campaigns with advanced customer segmentation and A/B testing.

## Features:
1. **Segmentation Engine**:
   - Segment customers by behavior, demographics, and engagement.
   - Save and reuse custom segments.

2. **Campaign Builder**:
   - Visual editor for email, SMS, and multi-channel campaigns.
   - Schedule and automate campaign delivery.

3. **A/B Testing**:
   - Test different messages and creatives.
   - Analyze results and optimize automatically.

4. **Analytics and Reporting**:
   - Track open, click, and conversion rates.
   - Generate detailed campaign performance reports.

5. **Integration**:
   - Sync with CRM and sales modules for unified data.

## Technical Stack
- **Frontend**: React.js for campaign builder and analytics UI.
- **Backend**: API endpoints for segmentation, campaign management, and analytics.
- **Integrations**: Email/SMS providers (SendGrid, Twilio).

## Deliverables
- Segmentation and campaign modules.
- Analytics and reporting tools.
- Documentation for users and admins.

## Timeline
- **Week 1**: Design segmentation and campaign models.
- **Week 2**: Develop backend and integrations.
- **Week 3**: Build frontend campaign builder and analytics.
- **Week 4**: Test, deploy, and document.
